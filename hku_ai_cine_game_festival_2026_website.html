🎬 Festival Theme

AI as collaborator. Human imagination amplified.

We invite creators who treat AI as a collaborator, not merely a visual engine, and who ask how algorithmic tools can extend human imagination, empathy and play. The festival foregrounds works that are formally inventive and emotionally resonant.

🌐 About the Festival Does

  • One-day flagship showcase of AI-generated films, demos, panels, workshops and awards reception.
  • Pre-event Game Jam for students and indie teams.
  • Public forum for technical, aesthetic and ethical dialogue.

✨ Programme Highlights

Screenings

Curated selection of AI-assisted films including narrative, documentary and experimental works.

Game & XR Expo

Playable demos, VR/AR experiences and interactive student showcases.

Panels & Keynotes

Conversations with creatives, researchers and industry practitioners.

Workshops

Hands-on tool clinics and responsible-practice labs.

Awards Ceremony & Reception

Celebrating craft, innovation and human–machine collaboration.

🗂️ Tracks & Formats

Film (Horizontal)

  • Narrative, documentary, experimental.
  • 3–15 minutes.
  • MP4 H.264 1080p recommended.

Vertical Videos

  • Portrait under 5 minutes.

Game & Interactive

  • Web, Windows/macOS, Android APK or VR builds.
  • Hosted or downloadable demo required.
  • Standard entry free.

📩 Submission

Submission Requirements & Transparency

  • Work link or playable demo.
  • 150–200 word synopsis.
  • Full credits and AI usage summary.
  • Ethics & rights declaration.

Eligibility & Core Rules

  • Must hold rights to all content.
  • All AI-derived elements disclosed.
  • No third-party IP infringement.

Judging Criteria

  • Artistic Quality 35%
  • Responsible AI Use 20%
  • Technical Execution 20%
  • Interactivity 15%
  • Ethics & Documentation 10%

Rights & Use

  • Non-exclusive license for screening & promotion.
  • Archival for educational research.
  • Festival does not claim ownership.

Technical Specs (Finalists)

  • Films: ProRes or H.264 1080p+.
  • Games: Windows/macOS preferred.
  • Vertical: 9:16 1080×1920+.
